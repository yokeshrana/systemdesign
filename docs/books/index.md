## About This Section

Use the embedded PDF viewer to read and bookmark important sections. Your bookmarks are saved locally in your browser.

### How to Use the PDF Viewer

1. **Read**: Navigate pages with the controls at the bottom.
2. **Bookmark**: Click the bookmark icon (⭐) to save a page.
3. **View Bookmarks**: Click the "Bookmarks" button to see all saved pages.
4. **Search**: Use Ctrl+F or Cmd+F to search within the PDF.
5. **Download**: Download the PDF to your device if needed.
